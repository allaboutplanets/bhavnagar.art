Rules for Competition

• Choose a category below and register on the link.

• Your painting should be on canvas or suitable art paper (i.e NO CHART PAPER)
• Submit your artwork by appointment to Phil Greene at Silver Bells Public School.
• Your original entries should be submitted no later than 30th August.

Age Group

Open to all age groups

☞ Dates for auction
The exhibition will be open to visitors on 5th to 12th September.The exhibition will be held at Nilambaug palace.
☞ Selection of Paintings
Artwork for exhibition will be selected in a preliminary round by a panel of local experts.
☞ About the auction?
All selected art pieces will also be included in an online and physical auction. The proceeds from a sale in the auction will be divided equally between the artist and Bhavnagar Heritage.
☞ About the Funds?
Funds raised from the auction will be used to further the great work that Bhavnagar Heritage does in our city
